THE BANKRUPTCY OF MAMMOTH LAKES, CALIFORNIA

On July 2, 2012, the mayor of Mammoth Lakes announced to an audience of 10 people that their town, just southeast of Yosemite, was filing for bankruptcy. Their dream of building a world-class resort was doing down. This is their story.
