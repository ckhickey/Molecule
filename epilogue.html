CREDITS


C.K. Hickey and Matt Sarnecki are students at UC Berkeley's Graduate School of Journalism with specialties in multimedia journalism.

C.K. has been vacationing in Mammoth Lakes for most of his life. In April 2012 his grandfather hinted that the town was in trouble. Upon further research and attending the town meeting when Mammoth declared bankruptcy, C.K. decided this was a story worth telling.

Matt Sarnecki led the reporting and video production.

C.K. Hickey led the immersive design and web development.
